Lets see how it works.

We connect you with travelers going places, if you both agree for the luggage transfer, leave each other a good review.

1 - Fill in

List your package details and when do you want it to be delivered. Wait till a traveler who matches your source and destiation expresses interest in taking it along.

2 - Say Hello

Chat with the intersted traveler directly by informing about the contents of the package and other details. As a benefit to the traveler, we suggest the sender to offer Pick and drop to the city from the Airport or train station.

3 - Post Review

Traveler and sender both post reviews about each other about the experience they had while dealing with each other.

Send It is for Everyone.

Traveling on leisure or going out for business, Take a piece of luggage for someone and let your luggage compartment pay for your airport transfers.
